How to Get Started?

Timeline Overview

Steps

  • Attend Information Session
    •

    Watch a short ‘First Steps’ video outlining study abroad at UC San Diego. Or attend an in person information session offered each week at the International Center. It is important that you complete a First Steps video or information session first to help you gain an overview of study abroad at UC San Diego

  • Meet Your Academic Advisor
    •

    Our advisors can help you find programs and answer your questions, regardless of where you want to go or what you want to study. You can make an appointment with an advisor by coming to our office or giving us a call at (858) 534-1123.

  • Find Your Program
    •

    You can go abroad and take classes in any major AND graduate on time! Even if your major is very specific or your 4-year plan is already completely full, we can help assist you in finding a program that will have applicable courses.

  • Apply
    •

    Complete the online application. See a list of application deadlines by country(PDF) or by date(PDF). Review the 10 Steps to Apply to UCEAP (PDF) instructions. First, go to MyEAP to begin your online application. Then go to TritonsAbroad, find your program, upload the necessary documents, and finish the application.
